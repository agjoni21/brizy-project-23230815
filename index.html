Kings Arms Motel Ocean City, MD

Your Beach Getaway Just Steps from the Ocean and Boardwalk

Welcome to Kings Arms Motel, conveniently located on 24th Street in Ocean City, Maryland, just block from the famous Boardwalk and Atlantic Ocean. Our motel is a long-time favorite for families and beach lovers who want clean, comfortable, and affordable accommodations in the heart of Ocean City.

Enjoy newly remodeled rooms featuring modern dcor, 42 flat-screen TVs, mini-refrigerators, microwaves, and free Wi-Fi. Guests also appreciate our daily housekeeping service, complimentary coffee, and free parking.

Relax by our outdoor pool and spacious sun deck, or take a short walk to local attractions like Trimpers Rides, Jolly Roger Amusement Park, Martys Playland, and Sportland Arcade. Youll also find popular Ocean City favorites nearby from Thrashers Fries and Carusos Pizza to Dumsers Dairyland ice cream.

With the Tanger Outlets, Assateague Island, Casino at Ocean Downs, and the Ocean City Convention Center all within minutes, Kings Arms Motel puts you close to everything Ocean City has to offer.

Book your stay today and experience the perfect mix of comfort, value, and location at Kings Arms Motel in Ocean City, MD.
